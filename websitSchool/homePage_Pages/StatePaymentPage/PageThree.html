    ASD, a member of the International Schools Group (ISG), operates as a not-for-profit organization within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, boasting a rich history of over fifty years. In the realm of international education, the not-for-profit model stands as the highest standard, with all generated revenue being reinvested back into the school. This approach signifies a commitment to excellence in education, as the school prioritizes the continual improvement of resources and facilities for the benefit of its students and the broader community, rather than pursuing financial gains.
